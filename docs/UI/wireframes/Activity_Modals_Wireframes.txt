### Functional Logic:
1. **The Pre-flight Gap**: 
   - Mandatory for activities to reduce starting friction and flow interruptions.
   - [Engage Focus 🚀] is disabled until all resource checkboxes are checked.
2. **Selective Focus**: 
   - Modals use the `Select` atom for category selection to prevent cognitive overload.
3. **Identity Anchor**: 
   - Meaning creation focuses on the "Why" (Personal legacy) to provide deep anchoring.
