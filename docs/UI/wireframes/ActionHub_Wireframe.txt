### Functional Logic:
1. **Low Energy Filtering**: 
   - Hides medium/high energy tasks.
   - **MANDATORY**: Always show URGENT tasks regardless of energy.
2. **Pre-flight Checklist**: 
   - Clicking [🎯 FOCUS] or [+] ADD opens a blocking modal.
   - Checklist items are pulled from the task's `resources` field.
