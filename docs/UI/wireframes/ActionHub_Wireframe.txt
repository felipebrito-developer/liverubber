### Functional Logic:
1. **Low Energy Filtering**: 
   - Hides medium/high energy tasks to prevent choice paralysis.
   - **MANDATORY**: Always show URGENT tasks regardless of energy.
2. **Activity Preparation (Pre-flight)**: 
   - Pressing [🎯 FOCUS] opens the **Pre-flight Check ✈️** modal.
   - This modal enforces resource verification and intention setting before the timer starts.
3. **Accordion Hierarchy**: 
   - Tasks are grouped by frequency (DAILY / WEEKLY / MONTHLY) to reduce visual noise.
