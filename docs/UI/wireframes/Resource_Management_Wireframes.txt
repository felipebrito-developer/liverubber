### Functional Logic:
1. **Tactile Adjustment**: 
   - [➕] and [➖] buttons perform a ±1 increment on the primary amount.
   - Updates are persisted immediately with a haptic pulse (if available).
2. **Metadata Edit**: 
   - Clicking [ ✎ ] opens the Management Modal for renaming or categorizing.
3. **Seeding Logic**:
   - New items start with "0" balance and are categorized under "Unsorted" if no category is provided.
